Almudena Grau

Spanish to English Freelance Translator


More than words. Understanding the underlying nuances of each message and finding the best way to convey them.



As Shakespeare wrote, “What’s in a name?”

Although ‘Almudena’ is a very traditional Spanish name, I am American at heart. My family relocated to the United States before my first birthday, and I grew up speaking Spanish at home and English everywhere else. Although I value and treasure the American way of life I experienced for so many years, there was always a part of me that pulled me towards my European roots.

So, around my 30th birthday, I set out on a new adventure and returned to Spain, where I currently reside in Alicante with my Spanish husband. I am completely bicultural and bilingual, but English is my native language and I only translate from Spanish to English.

Expertise

I have five years of project management experience at a localization agency in Washington DC (M2 Limited). This has given me insight on the issues faced by agencies and end-clients, allowing me to understand their expectations, challenges, and priorities, such as budgets, quality, and reliability.

It has also given me a technical and methodological mindset as to how translation projects should be planned, organized, and executed, and I have applied these aspects to my translation business since I established it in 2008.
